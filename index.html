Our Innovative Products

Logic Attendance Suite

Logic Attendance Suite is a comprehensive attendance and time management system designed for organizations of any size. It seamlessly records employee check-ins and check-outs, manages delays, overtime, leaves, and permissions, and generates detailed reports for HR and payroll. With a robust web dashboard and mobile app, TimePilot HR empowers companies to efficiently track and optimize workforce attendance, ensuring compliance and boosting productivity.

Attendance Tracking Employee Management HR Analytics Real-time Reports

EduNotify Voice

EduNotify Voice is a smart automated attendance and notification system for schools of all sizes. It records student check-in and check-out times, sends instant alerts to parents in case of absence, and features automated voice calls for student pickups when parents arrive. With seamless integration via mobile apps and dedicated devices, EduNotify Voice delivers detailed reports and ensures safe, efficient student management for modern educational institutions.
